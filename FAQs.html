For Corporate

Why should we try you?

Because we have an eye for detail both in terms of candidates and matching them with organization

What are your charges for various categories of services?

Recruitment- We charge a flat 15 days salary as commission. If a candidate joins at a salary of Rs50,000/- we charge a onetime commission of Rs.25,000/- + service tax
Payroll management- Let us have your query and we will get back to you within 24 hours with our quote
Trainings- Please tell us your requirement and we will get back within 24 hours with our quote alternatively if training needs are to be assessed send us a line of enquiry and we will meet you to discuss our terms
HR advisory services & HR consultancy - will be dealt with on a case to case basis, after we receive your query for the same. Quote will follow our discussions or visit
All other activities will be dealt with separately after we receive your specific query.

What if the candidate leaves after a short stay and after we have paid your commission?

We provide free replacement for 90 days, which means if the candidate was to leave on his/her own accord within 90 days of joining we will provide a free replacement

How can this be enforced?

We sign an agreement to this effect when we set off the process.

When is payment due?

We will submit our bill within 15 days of the candidate joining your organization. Payment is expected within 30 days. Any delay in payment will attract penal interest @10% pa.

What about disputes arising out of agreement if any?

All disputes to be settled within the jurisdiction of New Delhi.

How do we send a query and when can we expect a reply?

Please send us an e-mail at our address and we will get back in 24 hours.

For Associates

Who is eligible to become an associate?

Everyone, irrespective of caste, creed and sex, above the age of 18 with no upper age limit and no bar on qualification. People who are serving, retired, housewives, part timers, freelancers, individuals etc.

How does this associate relationship work?

We register any individual or agency/ firm with us by allotting an agency code. This is a 10 digit number that consists of your area pin code - and our associate code No. e.g. if you are based in New Delhi area, Green Park and if you have been allotted an associate code 1234 your full code N. will read as follows 110016 - 1234. The individual has to send us his name, contact details, address, pan card copy and bank details for on line payment.

I know of a company that has a vacancy what do I do?

You have to ask the company to send us their requirement through e-mail mentioning your associate code No. The following details must be evident in the e-mail-
  • Name of company and requesting official along with his/her designation and contact No.
  • Job title and brief description.
  • Salary offered (Minimum and Maximum range).
  • Qualifications and experience for the job.

Who fills the vacancy?

You can send CV's of friends, known associates, friends, colleagues etc. to us who are interested in the opening and we will short list candidates for the company. If you cannot manage this do not worry we will do this through our other associates.

What will be the payment outgo and when is it due?

For intimating a vacancy through the company you will be paid 30% of the commission amount (which is 15 days salary). This amount will be transferred to your bank account after the expiry of 90 days period.

Why can't I be paid earlier?

We have a legal obligation to replace candidates if they leave within 90 days, this obligation naturally is extendable to you.

What if I am not paid?

We will have an agreement between us at the time of becoming an associate which is enforceable through court within the jurisdiction of New Delhi.

How do I know I am due for payment?

Your associate code will be up on the site alongside the vacancy which will be valid and enforceable as per law.

What if I am able to raise a vacancy query and fill it with resume sent from my side?

In that case you will be due for payment on two counts - 30% for forwarding the vacancy and 30% for successful CV.

Is it necessary that an associate has to forward a vacancy and seek candidates to fulfill this vacancy?

No- you can do either activity or both.

Who does this role of filling up vacancies?

Our other associates will forward resumes which are vetted by us in accordance to requirement and forwarded to the company. Once a resume is selected and the candidate joins, this associate is entitled to his/her share of 30% of commission amount.

What happens if a vacancy is not filled?

We normally wouldn't allow such a thing to happen but if it were to happen all of us stand to lose.

What if I can manage a training assignment or other services listed?

Will be negotiated on a case to case basis in the most transparent manner.
